How it works

Score Differential

Each round's differential = (113 ÷ Slope) × (Score − Course Rating). Your index averages your lowest differentials from the last 20 rounds using the WHS sliding scale.

Rounds needed

1–2 rounds: shows a rough estimate (~) — just the average differential, no WHS adjustment.

3+ rounds: full WHS calculation kicks in, scaling from best-1-of-3 up to best-8-of-20.
