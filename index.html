How this works: each round's Score Differential = (113 ÷ Slope) × (Score − Course Rating). Your Index uses the WHS sliding scale — best 1 differential once you have 3+ rounds, scaling up to best 8 of your last 20. With 1–2 rounds the rough estimate shown is simply the average differential with no adjustment — treat it as a ballpark only. Ratings for Wildwood (blue tees) and Painted Woods (gold tees) are a reasonable starting point — check your scorecard and edit them in My Courses if they're off.
